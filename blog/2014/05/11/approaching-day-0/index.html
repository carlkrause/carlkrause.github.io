Approaching Day 0

May 11th, 2014

So in just over 12 hours I’ll be coding 80 hours a week. It’s kind of crazy. A year ago the idea of doing an intensive bootcamp to learn programming would have seemed crazy to me. But people change.

Phase 0 (the intensive preparatory phase) was a bigger commitment than expected. I put in 20-25 hours each week, learning Ruby, JavaScript, jQuery, Git, SQL, and HTML/CSS basics. It was a lot of ground to cover and while still a beginner, the amount of material I’ve learned is immense.
